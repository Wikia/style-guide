Buttons

Wikia forms for everyday use. You can have them just for $1. Buy now and you will get one radio button for free.

Standard

Wikia corporate buttons are used in most cases when the interface calls for a button.

Secondary buttons are only used when there is an exceedingly distinct and clear call-to-action. An example of this is the Vote button on a poll, or a Send.

Themed community buttons are similar to Wikia corporate buttons, but they they use different color rules (go into theming to check the rules).

Sizes of buttons depend on the situation. In most cases we use medium size.


Labels in buttons are aligned vertically and horizontally.

Icon Buttons

Left Side Navigation is a special case of an icon button. Behavior and interaction are the same.
